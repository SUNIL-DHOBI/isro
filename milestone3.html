ADITYA-L1 MISSION

The ADITYA-L1 mission, developed by ISRO and Indian institutions, is India’s first dedicated space-based solar observatory. Launched in 2023, it orbits the Sun-Earth Lagrange point 1 (L1), 1.5 million km from Earth. The mission studies the Sun’s chromosphere and corona, focusing on coronal heating, solar magnetic storms, and solar wind. Its advanced instruments provide continuous solar observations for understanding solar activities and their impact on space weather.
