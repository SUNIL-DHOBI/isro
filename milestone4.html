GAGANYAAN MISSION

Gaganyaan is India’s ambitious human spaceflight mission by ISRO to send a crew of three astronauts to low Earth orbit, 400 km altitude, for a three-day mission. The mission includes unmanned test flights followed by a manned mission planned for early 2027. It aims to demonstrate India’s indigenous capability in human spaceflight, focusing on crew safety, life support systems, and advanced space technology development.
